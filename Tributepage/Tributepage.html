MAGNIFICENT MARY

Olympic Medalist | 6x World Champion

M.C. Mary Kom

Against All Odds

Born in a humble family in Manipur, Mary Kom broke through cultural barriers with nothing but grit. She is the only woman to win the World Amateur Boxing Championship six times and the only female boxer to win a medal in each of the first seven world championships.

6 World Titles
2012 Olympic Bronze
Padma Vibhushan
"Don't let anyone tell you you're weak because you're a woman."

The Legacy Beyond the Ring

Today, she runs the Mary Kom Regional Boxing Foundation, providing free training to underprivileged youth, ensuring that the next generation of champions has the support she once lacked.
